1.1 Vision

Build a self-improving outbound engine that continuously:

Identifies ideal companies + contacts
Understands why they are good prospects
Generates high-quality, personalized outreach drafts
Learns from outcomes and improves automatically

The system runs continuously in the background and produces a ready-to-contact lead list with minimal manual work.

1.2 Core Goal

Output a structured dataset of:

Qualified companies
Relevant decision-makers
Verified contact info
Contextual signals (why now)
AI-generated personalized email draft

→ This becomes directly usable in tools like Instantly, Smartlead, or manual outreach.

1.3 What “Ready for Outreach” Means

A lead is “ready” only if all conditions are met:

Company level
Matches ICP (industry, size, geography, etc.)
Has at least 1 valid signal (e.g. hiring, competitor usage, growth)
Not already processed or excluded
Contact level
Correct persona (e.g. Head of CRM, Founder, Growth Lead)
Valid email (or high confidence)
LinkedIn profile (optional but preferred)
Data quality
No duplicates
Not previously contacted
Not blacklisted
Context
At least 1 clear reason to reach out
Supporting evidence (source link, extracted text)
Output
Email draft generated
Personalization included (company + signal-based)
Passes quality checks
1.4 Core Workflow (End-to-End)
Step 1: Define ICP

User defines:

Target industries
Company size
Geography
Personas
Competitors
Pain points
Signals to detect
Step 2: Discover companies

System finds companies via:

APIs / scrapers / datasets
Search-based discovery
Signal-based discovery (e.g. hiring, tools)
Step 3: Enrich companies

For each company:

Website
Industry
Size
Location
Tech stack
Signals (jobs, tools, reviews, etc.)
Step 4: Find contacts

For each company:

Identify relevant roles
Extract people (name, role, LinkedIn)
Step 5: Enrich contacts
Find email
Validate email
Assign confidence score
Normalize job title
Step 6: Extract signals

AI + scraping identifies:

Pain indicators
Competitor usage
Hiring intent
Growth signals
Relevant triggers
Step 7: Score leads

Each company + contact gets a score based on:

ICP match
Signal strength
Persona fit
Data completeness
Step 8: Deduplicate & filter

Remove:

Existing leads
Already contacted
Low-quality entries
Blacklisted domains
Step 9: Generate research summary

AI creates:

What the company does
Why it’s a fit
What likely problem exists
Outreach angle
Step 10: Generate email draft

AI produces:

Subject line
Opening line (personalized)
Body based on signals + ICP
CTA
Step 11: Quality control

System checks:

No hallucinated claims
Clear personalization
Not generic
Not spammy
Step 12: Output to deliverable

Final structured list:

Company
Contact
Email
LinkedIn
Signals
Score
Draft
Step 13: Feedback loop

After campaigns:

Import replies
Classify responses
Extract learnings
Update knowledge base

1.6 System Assumptions
Single user / single workspace
SQLite is sufficient for storage
External APIs will be used (replaceable)
AI is central for:
signal extraction
research
messaging
Pipeline runs asynchronously (not real-time UI driven)
1.7 Constraints
Technical
Backend: Python
Frontend: React
Database: SQLite
No distributed system (yet)
Must be modular (swap providers easily)
Data
External APIs may fail or be rate-limited
Data quality varies → must validate and score
Some signals will be probabilistic
AI
Risk of hallucination → must verify outputs
Cost constraints → batch processing preferred

1.9 System Architecture Principles
Modular

Each step is isolated:

Replace any module without breaking system
Pipeline-based

Linear but interruptible:

Each stage writes outputs to DB
Next stage reads from DB
Stateful
Everything persisted
No recomputation if already processed
Idempotent
Same run won’t duplicate data
Extensible

Future:

Multiple projects
Multiple ICPs per project
